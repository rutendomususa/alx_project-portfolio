About us

Move Along Investments (MAI) Private Limited is a wholly owned Zimbabwean company situated and operating in Harare. Move Along Investments was incorporated in June 2021 by the Companies Act, Chapter 24:31 Move Along Investments specializes in printing, painting & branding services, computers & computer consumables office stationery, corporate wear and protective clothing

Vision

To obtain and maintain a leading position in the provision of quality goods & services surpassing our customer expectations in Zimbabwe and beyond.

Mission Statement

Providing high-quality goods & services at affordable prices and delivery within a reasonable timeframe, using competent and professional staff, to satisfy customer needs across the globe.
